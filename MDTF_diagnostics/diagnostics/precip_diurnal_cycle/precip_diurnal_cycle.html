Diurnal Cycle of Precipitation

The diurnal cycle package generates a simple representation of the phase (in local time) and amplitude (in mm/day) of total precipitation, comparing a lat-lon model output of total precipitation with observed precipitation derived from the Tropical Rainfall Measuring Mission.
