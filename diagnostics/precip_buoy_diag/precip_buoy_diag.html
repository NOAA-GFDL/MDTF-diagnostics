Precipitation Buoyancy Statistics

The precipitation-buoyancy diagnostics module relates precipitation to a measure of lower-tropospheric averaged buoyancy and its two components: a measure of lower-tropospheric Convective Available Potential Energy (CAPE) and lower-tropospheric sub saturation (SUBSAT). The module evaluates the model precipitation sensitivity to thermodynamic variations by conditionally-averaging tropical oceanic precipitation by CAPE and SUBSAT, and visualizing the result in 3D as a precipitation surface. A metric that captures the CAPE vs. SUBSAT precipitation sensitivity is used to assess model performance compared to observations and a suite of CMIP6 models.
