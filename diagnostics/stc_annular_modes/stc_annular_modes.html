Seasonality and extremes of vertically propagating planetary waves

This POD does an EOF analysis on zonal mean geopotential heights to determine the annular modes (assumed to be EOF1) and their associated principal component time series. This POD assesses the annular mode EOF structures, and computes several annular mode diagnostics related to coupling with the stratospheric annular mode state.
